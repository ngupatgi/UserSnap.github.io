Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What payment options are available for the Usersnap subscription?

We accept payments via credit card such as VISA, MasterCard, and American Express. Please note that you do not need a credit card to sign up for a Usersnap trial. For Enterprise plans, we also accept bank transfers for annual plans.

Back to top

What should I do if my organization has no VAT-ID?

If you are a customer from the EU or UK, we require a valid VAT number. We apply the reverse charge system and you will not be charged for VAT on your invoice. Unfortunately, we can't offer you an account if your organization is in the EU (or UK) and you have no valid VAT number. If you set your country outside the EU (or UK), you will not be asked for a VAT-ID.

Back to top

What do "unlimited reporters" and "unlimited feedback items" mean?

Unlimited reporters are the people/visitors/customers who will send you feedback items (including screenshots) via your Usersnap feedback widget.

They can send an unlimited number of feedback items.

Back to top

What is a team member?

A team member is someone who has access to the Usersnap dashboard and can collaborate with other team members. Invite all your teammates.

In contrary, reporters are the ones who send in feedback via the widgets. They are not counted as team members.

Back to top

What is a feedback project? What are archived projects?

A project is a feedback widget for a use case with a dashboard and including a feedback item list.

Typical feedback widgets are: Internal Bug Tracking, Beta Test, User Feedback, Feature Feedback, Customer Feedback, Acceptance Testing (UAT), Customer Engagement, Integration Request, Net Promoter Score (NPS) etc.

If you purchase a plan in Usersnap, you can use all kinds of use cases. Your plan might also include some slot to archive projects, which you want to keep but you don't need them to be active.

Back to top

What is an integration and what 3rd party tools can I use?

Keep your existing software solutions, but enhance them with Usersnap to streamline your feedback process. Share the knowledge with your stakeholders and teammates within your organization.

With Usersnap, you can connect many 3rd party applications such as Jira, Slack, Asana, Azure DevOps, and others.

Through Zapier, you can connect thousands of applications and with the Webhook, generic interface, you can develop your own integrations. Don't stop within Usersnap, make Usersnap the center of customer insights for your business.

Customer service integrations like Zendesk are only available for company plan and above. Webhook integration is only available for Premium plan and above.

Can you tell me more about dashboard, team collaboration, notifications, custom data fields, multi-project inbox?

Dashboard: Log into Usersnap and find the easy-to-use dashboard. Everything is at the right place.

Team collaboration: Invite your teammates and collaborate right on each customer feedback item. Assign tasks, mention your colleagues to get their attention and dive into the voice of the customer together.

Notifications: Notifications help you to stay on top of the incoming customer feedback items. Whenever something important happens, you're live at the spot.

Custom data fields: Add hidden custom data fields to each feedback sent in. Add product versions, session information or other important data fields to each feedback item.

What is browser error recording? Can you record the console logs?

The Usersnap feedback widget can record all kinds of JavaScript errors from your users' browser. This helps your developers to understand why something didn't work. Reduce the work and effort of your team by automatically collecting essential technical information.

What is screen recording? Can my users record their screen with a video?

Offer your users the possibility to record their browser screen with a video and submit it with their feedback item. This explains what their issue with a user flow in your app is.

What is the feature customer care replies?

Collecting customer and user feedback involves to talk to your users. That's why Usersnap provides an easy-to-use customer reply feature (customer care) to answer via email within the Usersnap dashboard.

Your customers' responses will be shown directly at each feedback item. You don't have to switch to another software.

Can you tell me more about the development package with Client-side error logging, export feedback data, full API access?

Client-side error logging tracks all JavaScript errors that appear in the browser console log. Your developers will love that feature.

You can export all feedback items of each projects and use it in other environments.

With the full API access, you can not only customize the widget and it's behavior but also manipulate the feedback which is send in.

Can you tell me more about the widget features: Browser extensions, automated metadata, visual annotation methods, mobile website capture?

The Usersnap widgets offer various features depending on which use case they are solving for you.

Capture feedback via browser extensions and you don't need the widget code installed on your side.

Get automated metadata like browser size, operating system, browser type and country to avoid asking these questions to your users. You will streamline the feedback process.

Usersnap offers a variety of visual annotations methods like drawing, using arrows or selecting and commenting sections. That makes it easy for your users to show their issues instead of explaining.
